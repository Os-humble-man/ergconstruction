What We Do

Foundedin 2021 by Gloria Mwidya , a passionate and driven Congolese woman, our firm embodies a commitment to excellence, integrity, and love for our country. With a deep-rooted appreciation for the rich culture and potential of the Democratic Republic of Congo, Gloria's vision is to elevate the construction industry through ethical practices and unparalleled service.

At ERG, we believe that ethical conduct is not just a choice; it's our foundation. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of integrity in every aspect of our work. We approach every project with honesty, transparency, and a steadfast commitment to doing what's right, even when it's challenging.

Driven by our love for the profession, we at ERG bring a fresh perspective and innovative approach to quantity surveying. Our passion for the intricacies of construction projects, combined with our unwavering dedication to ethical principles, sets our firm apart as a beacon of integrity in the industry. At ERG, we are more than just consultants; we are partners in our clients' success. With our partners and expertise, we are committed to delivering exceptional service, fostering trusting relationships, and making a positive impact on our community.
